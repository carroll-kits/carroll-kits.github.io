Paper Plane
Launcher
Instructions

Follow along to build your very own paper plane launcher.

Materials

  • 2 Chopsticks
  • 6 Small Popscicle Sticks
  • 1 Rubberband
  • 1 Clothespin
  • Glue (not provided)
  • Scissors (not provided)
  • Tape (optional)

Step 1: Gather your materials and lay them out in your workspace. We recommend using cardboard or some other material to protect the table from getting dirty.

Step 2: Using a thin lines of glue, glue the six popscicle sticks on top of each other, one at a time, to form a single stack. After glueing each stick, hold the stack tightly for up to 1 minute so the glue can set.

Step 3: With your popscicle stick stack laying sideways, glue a chopstick to each side. Hold everything tightly for up to 1 minute so the glue can set.

Step 4: Glue the clothspin to the popscicle sticks as shown in the picture below. Wrap everything with a rubberband to hold it tight and let it dry for 30 minutes to 1 hour.

Step 5: Cut the rubberband and secure it to the tips of the chopsticks by either taping it, or tying each end with a knot.

  • If using tape, lay the rubber band flat against the chopstick, then wrap the tape around the rubberband tightly. We used electrical tape, but any strong tape should work.
  • If using a knot, tie a single overhand knot, being careful to not break the rubberband when pulling it tight. The knot may eventually come undone with use, so simply retie a rubberband on when this happens.
Make sure the rubberband can stretch back to the clothespin without breaking or coming loose.

Step 6: Using a sheet of paper, fold your favorite style of paper airplane. You may optionally put a piece of tape on the back to protect the paper from the rubberband. Secure the rubberband of the launcher into the clothespin, load the paper plane between the chopsticks, aim in a safe direction away from people, and squeeze the clothespin to launch!
